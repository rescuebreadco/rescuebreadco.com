Why Rescue Bread?

40% of food in the U.S. goes to waste. We turn surplus ingredients into delicious, nutrient-dense bread.

Meet the Baker

Hi, I’m Ujala Chauhan. I’m a student at New York University studying Nutrition and Food Studies and Environmental Science. Rescue Bread Co. began when I started experimenting with bread baking and realized how many ingredients we throw away. One day I turned carrot greens — something most people discard — into a savory garlic ciabatta. It was incredible. That moment sparked an idea: What if we could transform surplus ingredients into incredible breads instead of throwing them away? Now I bake breads that are: plant-based high-protein nutrient-dense made from rescued ingredients
